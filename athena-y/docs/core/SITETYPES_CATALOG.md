# ATHENA SITETYPE CATALOG

The Athena CMS Factory offers a wide range of sitetypes, categorized by various market niches.

## STRATEGIC FLEET (BLUE OCEAN 2025)
Hyper-specialized types addressing specific SME needs.

1.  **Arbor-Export:** Nurseries with export stock and root-type selectors.
2.  **GreenMaster:** Landscapers with before-and-after sliders and planting lists.
3.  **BuildFlow:** Finishing companies with real-time material price synchronization.
4.  **ArtisanConnect:** Bakers/Butchers with automated allergen labeling.
5.  **PracticePulse:** Healthcare practices with an evidence-based content library.
6.  **BevFlow-Pro:** Beverage wholesalers with Event Calculators and packaging matrices.
7.  **Memorial-Hub:** Funeral services with digital memorial pages and livestreams.

## CLASSIC FLEET
Proven types for various sectors.

*   **Medical/Dentist:** GPs, dentists, and medical centers.
*   **Hair/Wellness:** Salons and wellness centers.
*   **Restaurant/Food:** Local catering and takeaways.
*   **Store (Fashion/Payment):** Various e-commerce solutions.
*   **Portfolio/Blog:** Individual professionals and content creators.
*   **Academy:** Educational institutes and online courses.

## TIERS
Each type is available in 4 levels:
- **Basic:** Core information, essential and fast.
- **Medium:** Expanded with specific sections.
- **Extra:** Includes social proof (reviews, FAQ).
- **Luxury:** Full feature set with video, animations, and advanced tools.
